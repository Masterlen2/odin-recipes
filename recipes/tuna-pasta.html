Tuna Pasta

Description

For a quick and straightforward muscle-building meal, try this easy tuna protein pasta recipe. With just a few simple ingredients, it provides a hearty dose of protein and carbs to support your bodybuilding goals without sacrificing taste or time. Perfect for busy days when you need a nutritious meal in minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (5 oz) of tuna in water, drained
  • 2 cups pasta of your choice (such as penne or spaghetti)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: grated Parmesan cheese for topping

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the drained canned tuna to the skillet and break it up with a fork. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally, until heated through.
  4. Season the tuna with salt and pepper to taste, adjusting the seasoning as needed.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the tuna and toss everything together until well combined.
  6. Remove the skillet from the heat and transfer the tuna pasta to serving plates.
  7. If desired, sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top of each serving for extra flavor.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your easy tuna protein pasta as a quick and satisfying muscle-building meal.

This recipe serves 2 and can be easily doubled or halved depending on your needs. Feel free to customize it by adding your favorite vegetables or herbs for extra flavor and nutrients.
